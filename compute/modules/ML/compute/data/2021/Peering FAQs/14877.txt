About:
The Pennsylvania Research and Education Network (PennREN) is Pennsylvania's statewide research, education, and community network. Owned and operated by KINBER, PennREN serves members from Higher Education, K-12, Healthcare, Libraries, Government and Public Safety, and more. Additionally, KINBER utilizes its 1,800 mile fiber optic footprint to expand broadband access to underserved and unserved regions of Pennsylvania.

For more information about KINBER and its services, please visit KINBER.org.

For more information about the PennREN network, please visit NOC.PennREN.net
