Peering policy
The peering policy for the Opera AS 39832 and AS 21837 network is as follows:
ASN: 39832, 21837 (US)
The maximum prefix is 20.
The minimal announcement for IPv4 is /24.
The minimal traffic volume for direct peering is 1 Gbps (however, in justified cases, we can peer with less traffic).
The minimal traffic volume for direct line is 3 Gbps (however, in justified cases, we can peer with less traffic) - 10 Gbps LR optic path only.
You must designate a 24/7 contact, NOC or equivalent.
You must have prefixes set in the Internet Routing Registry (RADB preferred).
An MD5 password is optional.
Setting the default route to Opera is forbidden.
Opera reserves the right to suspend peering temporarily if any form of network abuse occurs, to identify and fix the issues.
Opera reserves the right to decline peering.
