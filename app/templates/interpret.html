Interpreting Your Results

The zip file emailed to you contains 5 files: ppc_data, an overlap map, and three graphs (diff, own, and ratio). More information on the different sorting strategies can be found in the glossary.

Overlap Map

The graph above showcases inter-ISP coverage area overlap. An ISP’s PoPs are used to draw a polygon. The overlap is a result of the common PoPs between ISPs.

ppc_data
